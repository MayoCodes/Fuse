FUSE

How to Play:

Enter number of pairs playing (each pair = 2 players)

Players sit in pairs diagonally across from each other

Hold phone on forehead, partner gives clues

TAP ANYWHERE on phone to change to next word

Pass phone around circle until timer ends

Pair holding phone when timer ends = ELIMINATED!

Scoring
When your partner guesses correctly, just keep playing! Points are tracked automatically by time survived.
Pro Tip: For the best fullscreen landscape experience, add Fuse to your home screen! Tap the Fullscreen button for instructions.
